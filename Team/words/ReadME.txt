Words
. A quick challenge for smart people. Try to guess the words in the given time and move to the next level.
. This game is developed using Html , CSS and JavaScript.

.How to play the game:
  + Open the index.html file and choose the game or directly open level1.html in the words folder.
  + Press start.
  + You will have 2 minutes to guess 5 words correctly and move to the next level.
  + You can reset the game again by pressing Reset button.
  + You can click submit to submit your guess and delete to reset the input field.
